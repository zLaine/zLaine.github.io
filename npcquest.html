Roles: Programmer, Designer
Engine: Unity
Language: Javascript
Team Size: 3
Duration: One semester
Created: Fall 2014


NPCQuest is a 3D RPG featuring puzzles, a turn-based battle system, and upgradable equipment. The battle system features unique mechanics for each character. As programmer I was in charge of setting up the GUI and battle system for this game, as well as implementing the leveling system. While working on the battle system I also created a basic AI for the enemies to follow. I assisted with the mechanics design for the battle system as well as contributing toward the game's concept.

Team members:
Kain Henry: Art/animation, Lead programmer, lead designer
Yan Rodriguez: Music/sound
